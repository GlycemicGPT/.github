GlycemicGPT is a full diabetes management platform that connects your CGM and insulin pump data with AI-powered analysis, real-time monitoring, and caregiver alerting. Self-hosted, privacy-first, no subscriptions required.

### Repositories

| Repo | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **[GlycemicGPT](https://github.com/GlycemicGPT/GlycemicGPT)** | Core platform -- API, web dashboard, AI sidecar, Docker stack |

*More repositories coming soon: Android (unofficial & Play Store), iOS, and the project website.*

### Get Involved

We welcome contributions from developers, designers, and people living with diabetes. Check out the [Contributing Guide](https://github.com/GlycemicGPT/GlycemicGPT/blob/develop/CONTRIBUTING.md) to get started.

### Roles

- **Contributors** -- anyone who opens issues, submits PRs, or joins discussions
- **Committers** -- trusted contributors with write access to specific components
- **Maintainers** -- project stewards responsible for releases and direction

See [GOVERNANCE.md](https://github.com/GlycemicGPT/GlycemicGPT/blob/develop/GOVERNANCE.md) for details on how to grow your involvement.
